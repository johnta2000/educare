Who we are:

Penn Educare is a pro bono student group at the University of Pennsylvania that creates original graphics and comprehensible resources for patients.

With our team of dedicated writers, graphic designers, and web designers, we collaborate with clinics and health organizations in the Philadelphia area to develop better educational resources.

Selective dorsal rhizotomy pamphlet

Our pilot project focused on developing a resource for a surgical procedure called Selective Dorsal Rhizotomy with a pediatric neurosurgeon and a team of neuro-rehabilitationists. One of our primary goals was to demystify the mechanics of the surgery since many patients and their families are worried that the surgeon is cutting the spine, whereas abnormal rootlets are cut instead.
