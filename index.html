Mission Statement:

Penn Educare is a student group at the University of Pennsylvania creating cutting-edge graphics and comprehensible resources for patients.

With our team of dedicated writers, graphic designers, translators, and web designers, we collaborate with clinics and health organizations across the United States to develop better educational resources.
