Our Mission

We are a student-led organization based out of the University of Pennsylvania developing cutting-edge health graphics and resources for our nine partners across the United States. Our team of writers, graphic designers, and translators seek to address important health questions asked by both our local community in Philadelphia and the general public by working with free clinics, community non-profits, and public health organizations.
